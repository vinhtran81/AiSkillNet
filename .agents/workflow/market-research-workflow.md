# Market Research Workflow

This workflow guides a comprehensive market research initiative to inform product strategy, feature prioritization, and go-to-market decisions.

## Phase 1: Research Scope & Objectives

**Goal**: Define clear research questions, target segments, and expected deliverables.

1. **Define Research Questions**: [Senior Product Owner](../skills/senior-po/SKILL.md) specifies the key questions to answer, such as:
  - What are the top 3 unmet user needs in our space?
    - Which competitors are gaining market share, and why?
    - What pricing models work best in our segment?
2. **Identify Target Segments**: Define the user personas and market segments to study (demographics, geographies, behavior patterns).
3. **Select Research Methods**: Choose from: competitor analysis, keyword/SEO research, user surveys, app store analysis, social listening, etc.

## Phase 2: Competitive Analysis

**Goal**: Map the competitive landscape and identify opportunities for differentiation.

1. **Competitor Identification**: [Senior Market Research Specialist](../skills/senior-market-research-specialist/SKILL.md) identifies direct, indirect, and emerging competitors.
2. **Feature Comparison**: Build a feature matrix comparing our product against top competitors across key dimensions (features, pricing, UX, platform support).
3. **SWOT Analysis**: Perform a Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats analysis for the product relative to the market.
4. **Review & Forum Analysis**: Analyze competitor ratings and reviews on platforms like G2, Capterra, or relevant industry forums to spot user pain points and trends.

## Phase 3: User & Market Insights

**Goal**: Understand user behavior, pain points, and emerging trends.

1. **Search Demand Analysis**: [Senior SEO Specialist](../skills/senior-seo-specialist/SKILL.md) analyzes search volume trends, rising keywords, and content gaps in the market.
2. **User Data Analysis**: [Senior Data Analyst](../skills/senior-data-analyst/SKILL.md) reviews internal analytics — retention rates, feature usage, funnel drop-offs — to validate market hypotheses.
3. **Social & Community Listening**: Monitor forums, Reddit, social media, and review sites for user sentiment, feature requests, and common complaints.
4. **Trend Analysis**: Identify emerging industry trends (e.g., AI integration, privacy-first features, subscription fatigue) that may impact strategy.

## Phase 4: Strategic Synthesis

**Goal**: Translate raw research into actionable product and marketing strategy.

1. **Opportunity Mapping**: [Senior Market Research Specialist](../skills/senior-market-research-specialist/SKILL.md) maps the most promising opportunities by plotting Impact vs. Effort for potential features or pivots.
2. **Positioning Strategy**: Define the product's unique value proposition (UVP) and positioning statement based on research findings.
3. **Content Strategy Alignment**: [Senior Content Writer](../skills/senior-content-writer/SKILL.md) aligns content topics and messaging with the discovered user needs and search demand.
4. **Pricing Insights**: Benchmark pricing models (freemium, tiered, one-time) against competitor data and user willingness-to-pay indicators.

## Phase 5: Reporting & Action Items

**Goal**: Deliver a clear, actionable research report and define next steps.

1. **Research Report**: Compile findings into a structured report including: Executive Summary, Key Findings, Data Visualizations, Recommendations.
2. **Backlog Prioritization**: [Senior Product Owner](../skills/senior-po/SKILL.md) updates the product backlog based on research insights. Create or reprioritize user stories.
3. **Design Implications**: [Senior UI/UX Designer](../skills/senior-uiux/SKILL.md) notes any UX patterns or design trends worth adopting.
4. **Stakeholder Presentation**: Present findings to stakeholders with clear recommendations and proposed roadmap adjustments.
